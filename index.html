About Me

Hi! My name is Adarsh and I'm currently an engineering student at the University of Pennsylvania. I've loved making things since I was little, whether it be software or hardware. From Arduino, to Rube-Goldberg machines, to machine learning, I've always sought after building new things. Nothing beats the feeling of completing a successful project, and each project presents so many opportunities to learn new tools and technologies, no matter how esoteric.

I have the same goals as any other engineer: build new things to solve complex problems. My approach is to take every opportunity as a way to not only create, but learn and grow as well.

Outside of academics and programming, I enjoy playing sports like basketball, and am a semi-avid runner. I'm a big fan of HGTV and can spend hours looking at houses and seeing people build things. I also am a fan of youtube, with my favorite channels being 3blue1brown, pocket83, CodeBullet, and BingingWithBabish.

If our interests align, feel free to reach out!
